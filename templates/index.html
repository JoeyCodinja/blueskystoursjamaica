Kingston Tour

Imagine exploring and experiencing Jamaica’s history, customs and traditions.

Join us as we tell the story of Jamaica’s Emancipation from slavery, heroes, politics, musical heritage and world class athletes. Come and view our National Parks, Art Institutions, main food market and eat lunch at one of our main food stops.

We guarantee that we will give you the true Jamaican experience as you explore the dynamics of Kingston, meet and mingle with its warm and friendly people and enjoy, the “Heartbeat of Jamaica”.

Port Royal Tour

Visit which was once called, the “Richest and Wickedest City in the whole world.”

Learn about the Buccaneers, the great earthquake, enjoy the town’s history, scenery and enjoy a delicious Jamaican seafood meal. Also view the Plumbline Light house and be renewed and revived at the Rock fort Mineral Bath.

Blue Mountain Tour

Take the trip up to our world re-nowned Blue Mountains where you will enjoy spectacular views.
Enjoy New Castle one of our army's bases, then visit Hollywell Botanical Gardens where you can hike, swim or just enjoy the beautiful garden with its indegenous flowers, fauna and birds. Then on to Cafe Blue for our world class coffee or just enjoy an authentic delicious Jamaican meal.
